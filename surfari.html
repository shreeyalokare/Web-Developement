SURFARI



Ready for the ride of your life?

Then you’ve come to the right place. Welcome to SURFARI!

At Wave Warehouse, we are delighted to have some of the most experienced surfers in the country at our hands to take you on your very own SURFARI down the Wild Atlantic Way.


From the silky soft beaches in Rossnowlagh, to the rough waves in Murvagh, to the deadly dunes in Strandhill, we promise to deliver an unforgettable experience.

Catering for individuals and groups, our instructor’s chauffer you around our decked out Range Rover cruiser, equipment and all provided, experiencing some of the best surf, popular attractions and other fun activities.

All you have to do is bring yourselves and a fun attitude!
SURFARI cost includes transfer to and from chosen destinations around the coast, equipment and meals. Cost may vary on choice of destinations- please don’t hesitate to contact us with any queries you may have.

Activities include


Surfing, Body Boarding, Stand Up Paddle Boarding, Dune Surfing, Zip Lining, Mountain Climb.
**Choice of attractions to visit includes three of the following-
Slieve League, Knocknarea, Glencar Waterfall, Eagles Rock, Gleniff Horseshoe, St Johns Point
COST- €70 per adult, €60 per child
