EQUIPMENT






Whether you are an experienced surfer or have completed a few lessons we have all the best equipment to get you out on the water.
If you are new to surfing, we strongly recommend that you try to complete at least two surf lessons before heading out and tearing it up on your own.
We can only rent when tidal conditions are suitable, so before travelling down it’s better to get in touch with us to avoid a wasted journey.
Rental is for two hours but can be extended for longer. A form of I.D needs to be left with a staff member as a form of deposit. (I. D’s accepted: Driving License, Passport, valid Student I.D)
We have recently purchased a new fleet of high-performance rental surf boards. We also have long boards to cater for our newbies.
In the winter, the wetsuit rental includes a winter wetsuit, winter boots and a hood.
In summer, the wetsuit rental includes a summer wetsuit and light shoes.



Prices
Wetsuit € 10
Surfboard € 15
Bodyboard € 10
Combo(wetsuit, board and boots) € 20
