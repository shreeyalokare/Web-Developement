WAVEY WAREHOUSE WEATHER W(R)EPORT







The key to a good surf is knowing the weather. Surfers must be aware of the weather as it decides the tide, waves, winds and swell.
**Don’t panic you don’t have to become a forecast junkie- we have it all laid out for you. **

WAVES


- LOW PRESSURE = marked with a L on weather map – generates large amount of swell aka GET OUT TO THE NEAREST BEACH
- HIGH PRESSURE = marked with a HIGH! – does not generate as much swell – aka if you’ve better things to do then Surf (not likely) you can skip the waves for now.

WINDS


- OFFSHORE WINDS (from land to sea) = Good surf conditions if partnered with swell
- ONSHORE WINDS (from sea to land) = Poor quality waves, not a great surf.

TIDES


Be very careful when identifying tides. They have a deadly effect on the break of a wave. We provide annual Tide Table in our Warehouse- containing information on tide times on the local areas.
Tide is 50 minutes to 1 hour later every day.
